# 2024年年度照片 Annual Album of 2024


Since college, I have been selecting my 10 favorite photos taken in the past year every January, documenting the places I have been to.

我自从大学以来，每年1月会评出我过去一年拍摄的10张我最喜爱的照片，记录我曾去过的地方。

This article will introduce the photos I took in 2024. Ranking is based on location, without distinguishing order.

本文将介绍我在2024年拍摄的照片。排名以地点为索引，不区分先后。
